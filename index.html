Bring back taxi and rideshare safety training in Toronto

On March 21, 2018, our little brother Nick was killed by an untrained and unsafe Uber driver. We believe Nick’s death was a direct result of City Council’s 2016 decision to scrap safety training requirements for both taxi and rideshare drivers.

Over 20,000 people have signed our petition so far and we are extremely grateful for everyone's support.

How you can help

  • If you haven’t already, sign and share our change.org petition.
  • Contact any candidates you are considering voting for using the list below.
  • Feel free to cc us at ridesharesafety@gmail.com.
  • If they have no stated position, ask them if they support rideshare safety.
  • If you get a response about a candidate’s position, email us and we’ll update this page.

Thank you

Any way you can help our campaign is a wonderful gift to our family, helping us turn this horrible tragedy into something positive by making sure the system won’t fail anyone else again like it did with Nick.

-Patrick and Rachel Cameron
